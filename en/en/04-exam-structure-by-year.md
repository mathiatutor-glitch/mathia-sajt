# FTN Entrance Exam — problem structure by year (Group A)

> Extracted from the official solved exams (ftn.uns.ac.rs). Group A = MATHEMATICS, 10 problems, 4 h. **The structure is almost identical year to year** — one problem per area. (Serbian original: `sr/04-struktura-po-godinama.md`.)

## Problem → area map

| # | Area | 2023 | 2024 | 2025 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Complex numbers | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| 2 | Quadratic + Vieta / irrational | ✅ | ✅ (irrational) | ✅ (quadratic) |
| 3 | Logarithms | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| 4 | Exponential | ✅ (inequality) | ✅ (inequality) | ✅ (equation) |
| 5 | Trigonometric equation | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| 6 | Vectors | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| 7 | Planimetry | ✅ (trapezoid) | ✅ (chord/inscribed angle) | ✅ (trapezoid) |
| 8 | Stereometry | ✅ (cone) | ✅ (pyramid) | ✅ (cone + inscribed sphere) |
| 9 | Function analysis (limit/derivative/integral) | ✅ | ✅ (limit + integral) | ✅ (extrema + limit) |
| 10 | Combinatorics | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |

## Takeaways (Group A)
- **All 10 areas appear reliably.** Safest "scoring" problems: complex numbers, quadratic/Vieta, logarithms, exponential, trigonometry.
- The **geometry block** (vectors, planimetry, stereometry) and **analysis** (limit/derivative/integral) separate stronger candidates.
- **Combinatorics** (#10) is often hardest — combinations/variations with repetition and the "bars and stars" method.

## Group B (P3–P6, 5 math problems)
- Verified 2025: quadratic (parameter + Vieta) · exponential · logarithmic · trigonometric · fifth from scope (sequence/vectors/combinatorics).
- **No** stereometry, analytic geometry, limits/derivatives, integrals.
